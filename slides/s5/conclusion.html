Conclusion

A cross-domain manifesto

Music data without context is useless

Linking your music data to other domains means you have many more ways to understand it

Making your data linkable means you enable the same benefit for other researchers

Your Linked Data maybe re-used in wonderful and unexpected ways

Linked Data is for Lazy People: leave the burden of ancillary descriptions to other communities of interest

The whole is greater than the sum of the parts

A cross-domain manifesto

Music data without context is useless

Linking your music data to other domains mean you have much more ways to understand it

Making your data linkable means you enable the same benefit for other researchers

Linked Data is for Lazy People: leave the burden of ancilliary descriptions to other communities of interest

The whole is greater than the sum of the parts

Personal Collection Management

Items in your collection can be linked to the corresponding Musicbrainz URIs

These can be found automatically using GNAT

Then, you can aggregate RDF data about your collection

Local collection → Musicbrainz → DBpedia → Geonames → etc.

It enables your media player (e.g. Songbird) to browse your collection in new and interesting ways
